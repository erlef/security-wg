Hex.pm recently completed its inaugural comprehensive third-party security
audit, made possible through Alpha-Omega funding as part of the OpenSSF's
commitment to strengthening critical open source infrastructure. The Erlang
Ecosystem Foundation's Ægis initiative coordinated the effort. Two independent
firms conducted the assessment: Paraxial.io handled white-box penetration
testing, while zentrust partners GmbH performed adversarial red-team
evaluations.
